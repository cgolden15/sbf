    User Guide

    Enter the quantity of each bill, coin, and coin roll.

    Set Starting Bank to your drawer's starting amount (typically $350).

    The Grand Total shows total cash counted. Net Deposit is total minus the bank.

    The Deposit Breakdown shows exactly which bills and coins to pull for the deposit.

    Click Net Deposit or Grand Total to copy to clipboard.

    Save & Reset saves to history and clears inputs for the next count.
